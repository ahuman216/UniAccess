Why Accessibility Matters

Over 1 billion people worldwide live with some form of disability. Traditional software often solves only one barrier at a time, is expensive, or ignores fundamental design principles.

UniAccess converts information across multiple sensory formats — text, audio, visual cues, and vibration — so every user can choose what works best for them.
